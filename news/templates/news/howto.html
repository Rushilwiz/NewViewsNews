How to use NewViewsNews


Here's how to get started with your new account on NewViewsNews.

Step 1

Take the political affiliation quiz through Spekr. This will allow us to get a gauge on the type of articles we should be suggesting to you.


Step 2

Write your first post. When logged in, you will see a write button in the top right corner of your page. From there you will see a form that you should fill out to make your article. Click here to write your first article!


Step 3

Once you have posted your article, click on the Home Page to see other members articles whose opinions may oppose yours.


Step 4

Continue broadening your political horizons on NewsViewsNews!
